# Primary references

Accessed 2026-07-13. Recheck living handbooks, schemas, correction policies, and provenance specifications before relying on version-specific behavior.

| Source | Publisher/owner and currentness | Governed topic |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [PRISMA 2020 statement](https://www.prisma-statement.org/prisma-2020) | PRISMA group; 2020 guideline published with 2021 statement papers | Transparent reporting of systematic reviews; not a generic web-search checklist |
| [Cochrane Handbook for Systematic Reviews of Interventions](https://www.cochrane.org/authors/handbooks-and-manuals/handbook/current) | Cochrane, current version 6.5 (2024) at review | Search, bias, synthesis, certainty, and reporting methods for intervention reviews |
| [W3C PROV-O](https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-o/) | W3C Recommendation, 2013-04-30 | Machine-readable provenance entities, activities, agents, and relationships |
| [FAIR Guiding Principles](https://doi.org/10.1038/sdata.2016.18) | Wilkinson et al., Scientific Data 2016, original peer-reviewed paper | Findable, accessible, interoperable, and reusable data/stewardship principles |
| [Crossref REST API](https://api.crossref.org/swagger-ui/index.html) | Crossref, living API documentation | DOI registration metadata lookup; metadata availability and accuracy vary by depositor |
| [Crossmark](https://www.crossref.org/services/crossmark/) | Crossref, living service documentation | Current status, corrections, retractions, and updates attached by participating publishers |
| [DataCite Metadata Schema 4.7](https://schema.datacite.org/meta/kernel-4.7/) | DataCite Metadata Working Group, released 2026-03-03 | Persistent-identifier metadata for research outputs and datasets |
| [C2PA specification 2.4](https://spec.c2pa.org/specifications/specifications/2.4/index.html) | Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity, newest listed version 2.4 at review | Signed content provenance and edit assertions; authenticity metadata does not establish truth |
| [RFC 7089: Memento](https://www.rfc-editor.org/rfc/rfc7089.html) | IETF Informational RFC, December 2013 | Time-based negotiation and discovery of archived web-resource versions |
| [COPE Retraction Guidelines](https://doi.org/10.24318/cope.2019.1.4) | Committee on Publication Ethics, version 2 published 2019 with living site guidance | Retraction purposes, notices, partial corrections, and publisher/editor responsibilities |
| [ICMJE Recommendations](https://www.icmje.org/recommendations/) | International Committee of Medical Journal Editors, updated January 2026 | Medical research conduct, reporting, authorship, corrections, and editorial practice |

These standards apply to their stated domains. Do not imply systematic-review, provenance, or publication-ethics compliance when the research process did not meet the relevant requirements.
